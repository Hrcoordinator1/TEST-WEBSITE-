About Our Clinic

Modern rehabilitation in a welcoming, patient-focused environment.

Our clinic is designed to support recovery through one-on-one attention, guided exercise, hands-on treatment, balance training, strengthening, and functional mobility care. We help patients of all ages improve confidence, reduce pain, and regain independence.

Our Services

Comprehensive care for every stage of your recovery.

Orthopedic Physical Therapy

Relieving pain and restoring movement for injuries and musculoskeletal conditions.

Neurological Physical Therapy

Specialized care for stroke, Parkinson’s, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological conditions.

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Guided recovery programs after surgery to help you heal safely and effectively.

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Relief from dizziness, balance issues, and vertigo.

Sports Physical Therapy

Injury prevention, performance enhancement, and return-to-sport training.

Pediatric Physical Therapy

Compassionate care to help children reach their highest potential.

Geriatric Rehabilitation

Support for strength, balance, mobility, fall prevention, and independence in older adults.
